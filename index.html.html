① Documents You Need — Sort These FIRST

Before anything else

⚠️ Get These Before Applying to Any Job

Without these documents, you cannot legally start work in Germany. Get everything sorted in your first weeks in Siegen. Some take time to arrive by post.

  • 🔢
    Steuer-ID (Tax Identification Number) — 11-digit number automatically sent to your registered address by post within 2–4 weeks of Anmeldung. Every employer needs this. If you lost it, request again at: bzst.de →
  • 🏠
    Anmeldung (Address Registration) — You must register at your Siegen address at the Bürgerbüro/Einwohnermeldeamt. Required for all employment. Brings your Steuer-ID by post automatically. Book an appointment: siegen.de
  • 🏥
    Krankenversicherungsnummer (Health Insurance Number) — Your public health insurance card number (from TK, AOK, Barmer, DAK, etc.). Required by employers for payroll registration. Enrol with a Krankenkasse as soon as you arrive — as a student, TK (Techniker Krankenkasse) and AOK are popular choices.
  • 🆔
    Sozialversicherungsnummer (Social Security Number) — Issued by Deutsche Rentenversicherung when you start your first job in Germany. Your employer registers you the first time. You can also request it proactively at: drv.de
  • 🎓
    Immatrikulationsbescheinigung (Enrolment Certificate) — Download from your Uni Siegen student portal each semester. Needed for Zenjob, staffing agencies, and most student job applications. Always carry a fresh copy.
  • 🏦
    German Bank Account (IBAN) — Required for salary payments. Open at Sparkasse Siegen, Deutsche Bank, or free online: N26, DKB, or Commerzbank (all free for students). Without a German IBAN you cannot get paid.
  • 📱
    German Phone Number — Required on all CVs. Cheapest options: Aldi Talk, Lidl Connect (~€10 for SIM + credit), or O2/Telekom contracts. Always put a German number on your applications — never a foreign one.
  • 🪪
    Aufenthaltstitel (Residence Permit) — Check that yours includes "Erwerbstätigkeit gestattet" (work permitted) or "Beschäftigung bis 140 Tage gestattet". Keep a photo of it on your phone. Every employer may ask to see it.

⑥ Production Worker / Factory Jobs

Siegen is an industrial city · Many options
💡 Siegen is historically an industrial city (steel, metalworking, automotive parts, plastics, packaging). This means there are many factories hiring Produktionshelfer (production helpers), Lagermitarbeiter (warehouse workers), and Montagehelfer (assembly helpers). No experience needed for most. Best approached via staffing agencies (Section 4) or directly on the job portals below.
  • 🔧
    SkinIdent GmbH — Siegen (57080 Siegen area) — cosmetics/skincare production. Confirmed Produktionshelfer listings. Search on karriere-suedwestfalen.de
  • ⚗️
    Messer Industriegase GmbH — Siegen (57074) — industrial gases manufacturer. Production and logistics helper roles. Apply via their website or search "Messer Siegen jobs".
  • 🔩
    Hess & Nies GmbH & Co. KG — Burbach (near Siegen, Siegerland) — plastics/metal assembly. Hires Montage/Produktionshelfer. Search on Indeed or 57jobs.de.
  • 🧪
    BISON Polymers — Siegen-Weidenau — already covered in Section 5. Also classified as production work — glove manufacturing. Great option with WhatsApp apply.
  • 📦
    Search keywords to use on job portals: "Produktionshelfer Siegen", "Lagermitarbeiter Siegen", "Montagehelfer Siegen", "Aushilfe Produktion Siegen", "Helfer Verpackung Siegen". Use these on: 57jobs.de, karriere-suedwestfalen.de, and Indeed.de

⑬ Online & Remote Jobs

Work from home · Flexible
⚠️ Important for non-EU students: Self-employment/freelancing requires Ausländerbehörde approval. However, working as an employee remotely (e.g. Werkstudent remote, tutoring platform contracts) is fine. Always use platforms that issue a proper employment contract, not just invoice-based work.
  • 🎓
    Tutoring (Nachhilfe) — Teach school subjects to German kids. Platforms: Tutormatics.de (€15/45min), Superprof.de, Nachhilfe.de, Schülerhilfe (has Siegen branches). Also post flyers at the Uni — families hire students directly.
  • 🌍
    English Teaching Online — If you're a fluent English speaker. Lingoda (proper employment contract), AmazingTalker (set own rate). Pays €15–25/hr.
  • 🖥️
    Werkstudent IT/Tech (Remote) — If you study CS, engineering, or business: search "Werkstudent remote" on LinkedIn, StepStone, Indeed. €12–18/hr and great for your CV.
  • 🔬
    Clickworker / Appen — Data labeling, web research, AI training tasks. Clickworker.com and Appen.com. Flexible but pay is variable — best for filling 1-2 hour gaps between lectures.
  • 🎓
    HiWi / SHK at Uni Siegen — Student assistant jobs at the university. Best option: paid, builds academic CV, does NOT count toward your 140-day annual limit. Check: jobs.uni-siegen.de/HK →

⑰ Pro Tips & Walk-In Strategy

  • 📅
    Go walk-in between Tuesday–Thursday, 2–4pm. Avoid Mondays (busy), lunch rush (12–2pm), and Friday evenings. Managers are most available mid-afternoon on weekdays.
  • 📄
    Always carry 5–10 printed copies of your CV in German. Include German phone, Siegen address, university name, and degree field. Even if they say they don't need it, leaving a copy makes you memorable.
  • 🗣️
    The magic walk-in phrase: "Ich suche einen Minijob oder Nebenjob. Haben Sie gerade eine offene Stelle?" — This is exactly what they need to hear.
  • 🔄
    If they say no — ask: "Kann ich in 2 Wochen nochmal vorbeikommen?" — then actually come back. Persistence and showing up again is often what gets you the job.
  • 🍛
    Asian and South Asian restaurants (Indian, Chinese, Vietnamese, Korean) are especially international-student-friendly. Dishwashing and kitchen helper roles rarely require German — just show up and be reliable.
  • 🏢
    Staffing agencies are underused by students. Walk into 2–3 agencies in Section 4 with your CV and documents. They can place you within days at local companies — often faster than any online application.
  • ⚡
    Register on Zenjob first while you're doing everything else. It takes ~1 week to get verified but once active, you can book shifts whenever you have free time alongside a main job.
  • 📱
    For BISON Polymers — just WhatsApp +49 160 2813812 right now. Send your CV as a PDF. This is genuinely the easiest employer on this list to reach.
  • ⚠️
    Red flags to watch for: No written contract, pay below €13.90/hr, pressure to work more than 20h/week during semester, no Lohnzettel (payslip). Any of these = report to Arbeitsgericht (labour court) or Faire Integration.
  • 🎯
    Priority order for your first week: 1) Get your documents sorted 2) Register on Zenjob 3) Walk into 2 staffing agencies 4) Walk into BISON Polymers 5) Walk into nearby supermarkets 6) Apply online on DPD / Mensa portals.
